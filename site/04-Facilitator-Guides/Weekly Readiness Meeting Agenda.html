Build with us: Support & Knowledge Base for Beginners

A standard, repeatable agenda for the weekly readiness meetings (Weeks 1–4). These meetings are collaborative by design — we succeed together. Everyone is encouraged to share what's working, ask questions openly, and help one another clear blockers so no team is left behind. The objective every week:

Eliminate business and technical barriers before event week.

Standard Agenda (60 minutes)

Time Duration Segment
0:00 – 0:10 10 min Welcome & Week's Focus
0:10 – 0:25 15 min 1. Pre-Skilling Review
0:25 – 0:40 15 min 2. Technical Readiness Review
0:40 – 0:55 15 min 3. Business Readiness Review
0:55 – 1:00 5 min Action Items & Close

Welcome & Week's Focus

  • Remind everyone this is a collaborative community — we succeed together, share openly, and help each other clear blockers so no team is left behind
  • Invite everyone to share technical discoveries and lessons learned
  • Surface any emerging use cases
  • Raise open questions and challenges for the group to help with
  • Quick recap of last week's action items
  • Set the focus for this week's meeting

1. Pre-Skilling Review

Confirm progress on the Getting Started curriculum and readiness pages.

  • Who has completed the pre-skilling curriculum?
  • Any questions or sticking points on the readiness pages (Azure, VS Code, Python, Copilot)?
  • Note anyone falling behind and why

2. Technical Readiness Review

Walk the four readiness areas and clear blockers.

  • Azure — subscription access verified?
  • Azure AI Foundry — can create/open a project?
  • VS Code — installed and launching?
  • Python — installed with working pip?
  • GitHub Copilot — Pro or higher active in VS Code?
  • Log new blockers with issue, owner, and status
  • Flag anything needing IT/admin approval so we can plan around the timing

3. Business Readiness Review

Refine each org's use case and knowledge base.

  • Problem statement clearly defined?
  • Desired outcome and success criteria documented?
  • Example questions (should work / should not work) identified?
  • Knowledge base documents available, current, and accurate?
  • Update the Red / Yellow / Green self-assessment

Action Items & Close

  • Assign the highest-priority action item for each org
  • Confirm owners and due dates before next week
  • Remind everyone to post blockers and questions in the Teams community

Facilitator Notes

  • Keep the tone collaborative and encouraging.
  • Any unresolved gap becomes the highest-priority action item for the following week.
  • Update the shared blocker log immediately after the meeting.
